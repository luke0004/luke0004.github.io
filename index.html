Focused Apps

Apps that do one thing well.

SailorsLog A logbook for sailors. Capture passages, crew, weather, and photos. Relive voyages with interactive maps and GPX tracks. Share your journey with the people who care. Runs on iOS.
KlarText A plain text editor built on one rule: if a feature isn't essential to writing, it doesn't belong. No syntax highlighting, no plugins, no AI. Just text. Native MacOS.
Quay MacOS menu bar app. Sync to GitHub easily. Write in KlarText. Quay handles the rest.
Crossing A sailing simulation game. Early access on itch.io — realistic wind and tide physics, lots of traffic, and the challenge of crossing open water. TypeScript, Godot engine, web-playable.
Keel Digital humanities research engine and database. Proof-of-Concept for offline database sync and MCP.
